About

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at Western University and an incoming MD student at Stanford. My research integrates machine learning, image analysis, and anatomical coordinate systems for brain mapping and deep brain stimulation. I'm also passionate about global health equity, open-source medical software, and advancing stereotactic neurosurgery through interpretable and reproducible methods.
