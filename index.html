About

I am a PhD candidate in Biomedical Engineering at Western University, specializing in machine learning, neuroimaging, and brain coordinate systems for deep brain stimulation. I'm also an incoming MD student at Stanford School of Medicine. My work spans stereotactic targeting, quality control tools, and open science. I am also passionate about equity-oriented clinical research in neuromodulation and orthopedic surgery.
